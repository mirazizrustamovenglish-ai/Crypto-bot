## ✨ Asosiy Imkoniyatlar

- 📊 **10+ Texnik Indikator**: RSI, MACD, ADX, EMA, Support/Resistance, OTE
- 🏢 **Fundamental Tahlil**: Market cap, social media, developer activity
- 😨 **Sentiment Tahlil**: Fear & Greed Index
- 🔍 **Kuchli Filtrlar**: Faqat yuqori sifatli signallar (8+/10 ball)
- 💰 **Risk Management**: Avtomatik Entry, Stop Loss, Take Profit
- 📱 **Telegram Integratsiya**: Real-time signal yuborish
- 🌐 **24/7 Monitoring**: 10+ kripto valyuta (BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL va boshqalar)
- 🆓 **100% BEPUL API**: CoinGecko, Binance, Alternative.me

## 📋 Filtrlar Tizimi

Bot faqat quyidagi barcha shartlarni bajaradigan signallarni yuboradi:

1. ✅ **Hajm filtri**: 24h volume > $2M
2. ✅ **RSI filtri**: Overbought/Oversold holatlarni cheklash
3. ✅ **ADX filtri**: Trend kuchi > 25
4. ✅ **OTE filtri**: Optimal kirish zonalari
5. ✅ **Score filtri**: Signal bali ≥ 8/10
6. ✅ **Likvidlik filtri**: Market cap > $5M
